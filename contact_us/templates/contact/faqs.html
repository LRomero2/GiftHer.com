Frequently Asked Questions

To be acceptable for return, items should be unused, unopened and have any original tags and seals intact.
To initiate a refund, please return the items to the return address on your Dispatch Note.
Please ensure you retain proof of postage for any items that you return.
Refunds will be processed within 48 hours of receipt of the returns.

Please note: Gift Her will only reimburse the cost of the products and not any postage or shipping fees.

We are working hard to get all orders dispatched as quickly as possible. We expect orders to be dispatched within 2 working days of your order. The standard delivery time for our delivery partner is 3 working days, but please note that this may be longer during busy periods.

Our aim is to process your order as soon as possible, so there is limited time for us to be able to amend your order. Please get in touch to discuss your order with us.

If there is an item missing from your order, or if we have delivered the wrong items, please fill in a contact us form and report this to our customer services department.
